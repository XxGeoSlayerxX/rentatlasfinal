Apartment hunting in Montréal, made easy

RentAtlas helps you compare neighbourhoods based on a multitude of factors ranging from transit access, safety, walkability, job access, parking conditions as well as overall living costs. Open the map to see everything at once.

Open the Map →

What RentAtlas shows

  • Overall neighbourhood “apartment suitability” score
  • A basic parking index (vignette vs. free street parking)
  • Transit and job-access indicators

How to use the map

Go to the Map page, enter some of your most important factors for renting, then pan around Montréal and click on areas to see their scores. Later, you’ll be able to toggle between indicators like safety, parking, walkability, transit and jobs, in order to find the area most taillored to your current needs.

Behind the scenes

This site is built from open data and simple spatial analysis. As it grows, we’ll document data sources composing each layer, the parking index and the “extra living cost” estimates so you can understand what each score really means.
