Find the right neighbourhood, faster.

RentAtlas helps renters compare neighbourhoods using safety, parks, transit access, and parking, all while tailoring the results to their priorities. Explore the interactive map with its sliders to discover areas that match what matters to you.

Open the RentAtlas

Why this project

Finding a place to live is one of the most important decisions in life, and renters often make tradeoffs without clear information. RentAtlas consolidates public data into an intuitive map so you can quickly compare neighbourhoods by postal code for overall safety, greenery, transit access, ease of parking and hopefully much more very soon.

How it helps

Adjust the sliders on the sidebar to prioritize what matters, like parking over transit, and the map updates instantly. Use the top matches to discover FSAs that better match your needs. (FSA's are forward sorting areas, or the first 3 digits of the postal code)

Open data and made to improve

Data is prepared with Python notebooks and the processing steps are transparent so anyone can audit or improve the scores. In the future, live pricing can be implemented to each area in order to facilitate decision making. The goal is also to bring this to many more cities and serve more people.

Get started

  1. Open the interactive map and explore top matches.
  2. Drag the sliders to reflect your priorities.
  3. Click a neighbourhood to inspect the component contributions.
